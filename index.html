JMyDJ





J’myDJ is a group of college age DJs based in Athens Georgia whose mission is simple: bring the party. This past year we were the DJs for many senior Homecomings and Proms. School administrators love us. Our performances are clean, our prices are competitive, and the kids have a blast.
